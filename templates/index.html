    A NASA SPACE APPS CHALLENGE PROJECT

    "Will It Rain On My Parade?" was born from the NASA International Space Apps Challenge. Our mission is to make complex climate and weather data accessible, useful and engaging. We believe that with the power of open data we can help people make better decisions and plan for a brighter future.
